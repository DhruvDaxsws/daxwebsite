# **App Name**: ConsultFast

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Visually appealing homepage featuring services, case studies, and team introductions.
- Services Overview: Dedicated pages for each consulting service, detailing scope, approach, and benefits.
- Case Studies: In-depth case studies showcasing successful projects and client outcomes.
- Team Profiles: Individual profiles of consultants, highlighting expertise and experience.
- Contact Form: Easy-to-use contact form for inquiries and consultation requests.
- AI-Powered Consultation Suggestion Tool: Tool to provide tailored consulting service suggestions based on client-provided business descriptions using generative AI.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Bright blue (#00B5FF) to convey innovation and trust.
- Background color: Light blue (#E5F6FF), subtly desaturated from the primary to ensure balance.
- Accent color: Deep navy (#002A45) for a professional and reliable feel, used for headers and key details.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' sans-serif, lending a geometric, precise, contemporary look.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif, for a modern, neutral, machined, objective, look.
- Use modern, minimalist icons to represent services and solutions.
- Clean, modern layout with ample white space and clear calls to action.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience.
